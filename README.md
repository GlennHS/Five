## What Is Five?

FIVE is a  [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) project created by [GlennHS](https://glennhs.co.uk) to promote self-care, better habits and useful insight into what people are doing that is helping (and harming) their health. Not just mental or physical but all kinds of health. I believe there are at least FIVE main kinds of fitness everyone should try and keep healthy:

- **BODY**: Your **physical** health. This is your heart health, your "7 minute mile", your "my knee shouldn't make that noise" metric.
- **MIND**: Your **mental** health. This is your mind's health, letting your brain relax, switching off or challenging it to grow and stimulating it (positively).
- **WORK**: Your **academic/career** health. This can be climbing the corporate ladder, personal projects, learning something new, studying, doing that essay you were meant to do.
- **CASH**: Your **financial** health. This is how healthy your wallet is, boosted by things like saving and spending responsibly.
- **BOND**: Your **social** health. This can be caring for your friends, making time for your family or showing your partner you care; nurturing relationships boosts this metric.

FIVE is based off of a few founding principles:

- **If you're not growing, you're drifting**: *Entropy is awful but it's a fact of life. If you don't keep actively trying to better yourself you'll stagnate.*
- **Any effort, however small, is still effort**: *As long as you're actively trying to better yourself, however small, that should be praised and celebrated.*
- **No shame, only encouragement**: *You should always be able to look at FIVE and be proud of what you've achieved or feel inspired to do more for yourself.*
- **No paywalls**: *Why the heck would I want to price people out of self-care? If I had to pay for hosting or something, sure, maybe then. But not now.*

## What can you do with FIVE?

- Log daily actions across BODY, MIND, WORK, CASH, and BOND
- Track streaks and consistency over time
- See how your habits trend across different areas of your life
- Add new actions to track and tags to organise your actions

## Deploy FIVE yourself

If you want to run FIVE yourself on your machine just do the following:

- Clone the repo with `git clone https://github.com/glennhs/five` or however you normally clone stuff
- Install the dependencies with `pnpm install`
- Run `pnpm dev` to start the dev server!
